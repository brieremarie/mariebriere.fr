Short Presentation

I am Head of the Investor Intelligence & Academic Partnerships at Amundi Investment Institute in Paris. In this role, I conduct research on portfolio choice, with a focus on sustainable finance, household finance, pensions and the impact of financial technology, to advise the strategic decisions of institutional investors and the design of investment solutions for individual investors.
I am the Chairman of the Institute for Quantitative Investment Research (Inquire Europe), Scientific Director of the FaIR program at Institut Louis Bachelier and Chairman of the Scientific Committee of the European Savings Observatory, a member of the expert group advising the ESMA Risk Standing Committee and a member of several scientific councils, such as the French supervisory authority ACPR and the European Capital Market Institute of CEPS. I am also also a senior associate researcher with Université Libre de Bruxelles and Paris Dauphine PSL University and serve as Editorial Board member of the Financial Analyst Journal.
My scientifc articles have been published in academic journals and featured in several news outlets including the Financial Times and the Wall Street Journal. I hold a PhD in Economics from the University Paris X and graduated from ENSAE.
